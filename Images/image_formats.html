The Three Main Image Formats Used In The World

PNG:

  • Their are two types of PNGs

  • PNG-8 is like GIF, with only 256 colors to use

  • PNG-24 gives a higher quality on the image and is better at being transparent then a GIF

    JPEG:

  • JPEG's are great with photographs when there are shadows and other complicated things on the image

  • JPEG has a variety on colors

  • For JPEG's they only need to be adjusted with the quality of the image shown

    GIF:

  • GIF only has 256 colors, so they should not be used too much

  • GIF's work great when there is not a lot of shading and solid colors are used to fill big spaces

  • GIF's are meant to be simple images
