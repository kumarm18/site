There are five components of a good citizenship

  • Honesty is important to a good citizenship because you should be truthful to your country.
  • Compassion is important to a good citizenship because you should care for the people of your country.
  • Respect is important to a good citizenship because you should respect the people of your country.
  • Responsibility is important to a good citizenship because everyone should do their part.
  • Courage is important to a good citizenship because you should stand up for wht you believe in.

  • Something else that is important to a good citizenship is trust because you should trust the people in your country.
